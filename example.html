You can also concatenate jfleeckr to add photos inside the same div. Example: 

$("#flickr").jfleeckr({
			apikey: 'a43d0f79bbc56d823b039553379e28f0',
			extra: 'photoset_id=72157623977103845',
			method: 'flickr.photosets.getPhotos'
		}).jfleeckr({apikey: 'a43d0f79bbc56d823b039553379e28f0'});


That means: append 20 pictures from the photoset, and then append panda's random selection to the same div#flickr
